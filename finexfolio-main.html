Тrading

Managed Accounts

You trade on your own account while your positions are copied to the investor accounts according to the set allocation type. Profits and losses are also allocated to the investor accounts according to the allocation type.

Hedge Fund

You trade on a single account composed of the funds on your investors' accounts. Profits and losses are allocated in proportion to the invested funds.

Number of investors

Managed Accounts

The maximum number of investors may be limited by the prime broker.

Hedge Fund

The maximum number of investors may be limited by the prime broker.

Order execution

Managed Accounts

The opening/closing prices of the trading instruments may insignificantly differ on the investor accounts as the orders are executed individually on each investor account.

Hedge Fund

The same buy/sell prices for each investor, market order execution.

Allocation type

Managed Accounts

You can set the allocation type individually for every investor:
- Proportionally by balance
- Proportionally by equity
- Proportionally by free margin
- Fixed lot allocation
- Ratio allocation
- Proportionally by equity × multiplier
- Proportionally by balance × multiplier

Hedge Fund

Profits and losses are distributed in proportion to the invested capital.

Possibility to pause trading activities on the investor account

Managed Accounts

You can start or pause the trading activity individually on each investor account.

Hedge Fund

Trading activity cannot be paused on an individual investor account.

Management of deposits and withdrawals

Managed Accounts

The funds are instantly deposited to or withdrawn from the investor account.

Hedge Fund

You can decide when the funds are deposited to or withdrawn from the investor account. Either instantly, or you can set a schedule or you can approve deposits and withdrawals manually.

Option to partially buy an instrument (e.g. DAX)

Managed Accounts

It's possible.
